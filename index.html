Who am I?
An Englishman Who lives in Germany
Originally from a retail background I took a sabbatical and a risk
To learn web development And software engineering
With a passion for food, baking and all things tech

I'm a career changer, originally from a retail background I took an opportunity an old employer gave me for a sabbatical. To begin with I wanted to travel and enjoy my time off, moving in with my long distance girlfriend in Germany, but it got me thinking, do I want to continue in this field forever?

The short answer was NO. I Looked around for different career choices and opportunities, and finally found something that I'd had an interest in since college. I took a risk and decided to pay for a software development bootcamp. Having recently graduated I'm a fully qualified Fullstack Web-Dev! Who is now open to opportunities in the startup scene in Berlin.
